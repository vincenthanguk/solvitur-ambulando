What is Solvitur Ambulando?

Solvitur Ambulando is a mindfulness application that allows you to log your personal walking journey.

To get started journaling your walks, make sure to activate your browser's location services. Then simply click on the map, choose the type of walk and enter your walking distance and duration. Solvitur Ambulando will then calculate an approximate step-count and store the data in your browser.

It is scientifically proven that walking soothes the mind and helps you come up with great ideas. In order to aid this process, you may quickly jot down some thoughts that crossed your mind before, during, or after the walk. What is worrying you? What are you thankful for? What amazing thing have you experienced on your daily stroll? What's the meaning of life, anyways?

With Solvitur Ambulando you can check at a glance, which parts of your neighborhood you have already explored and the built-in journaling function allows you to re-experience the awesome memories and profound insights you have encountered along the way.

What are you waiting for? Get out there, explore the world - and yourself! With Solvitur Ambulando!
